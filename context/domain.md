# Domain Knowledge

## Business Context
B2B SaaS company selling a collaboration platform to mid-market and enterprise customers. Revenue model is annual subscriptions with seat-based pricing. Go-to-market motion is product-led growth (PLG) supported by an outbound sales team for enterprise deals.

## Key Metrics & Definitions

### Lead Funnel
- **Lead**: Any identified contact who has engaged with marketing (form fill, content download, webinar, demo request)
- **MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead)**: Lead with a lead score >= 80, based on firmographic fit + behavioral signals
- **SQL (Sales Qualified Lead)**: MQL accepted by sales after initial outreach — confirmed budget, authority, need, and timeline
- **Opportunity**: SQL converted to a deal in the pipeline with an estimated close date and deal size

### Pipeline & Revenue
- **Pipeline**: Total dollar value of open opportunities (Stage 2+)
- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**: Annualized contract value of active subscriptions
- **Bookings**: New ARR added in a period (new business + expansion - contraction - churn)
- **Win Rate**: Opportunities closed-won / total opportunities that reached Stage 2+
- **Sales Cycle**: Days from opportunity creation to closed-won

### Marketing Performance
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Total sales + marketing spend / new customers acquired
- **Campaign Attribution**: First-touch and multi-touch attribution models; first-touch assigns 100% credit to the campaign that generated the lead, multi-touch distributes across all touchpoints
- **Channel**: Source of lead generation — paid search, organic, events, outbound SDR, partner, PLG (product sign-up)

### Segments
- **SMB**: < 200 employees
- **Mid-Market**: 200-2,000 employees
- **Enterprise**: 2,000+ employees

## Fiscal Calendar
- Fiscal year starts February 1
- Q1: Feb-Apr, Q2: May-Jul, Q3: Aug-Oct, Q4: Nov-Jan
