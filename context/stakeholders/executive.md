# Stakeholder: Executive Team

## Team Mission
Set company-level strategy and drive profitable growth by aligning sales, marketing, product, and customer success around shared targets.

## Key Responsibilities
- Own revenue targets: ARR, bookings vs plan, net revenue retention
- Report to the board on business health and growth trajectory
- Allocate budget across departments based on ROI and strategic priorities
- Monitor unit economics and ensure sustainable growth

## What They Care About
- ARR and ARR growth rate
- Bookings vs plan (new business + expansion)
- Net revenue retention (NRR)
- LTV/CAC ratio by segment
- Pipeline coverage ratio vs quarterly target
- Gross margin and unit economics
- Revenue mix by segment and product line
- Customer count and logo churn

## How They Use Data
- Monthly board reporting with QoQ and YoY comparisons
- Quarterly strategic planning and budget allocation
- Weekly leadership reviews focused on bookings pace and pipeline
- Ad-hoc deep dives when metrics are off-track

## Communication Preferences
- Lead with the headline number and whether we're on/off plan
- Always compare to plan, prior period, and prior year
- Use precise financial figures — round but don't obscure
- Board-level summaries: 2-3 key takeaways, not 20 metrics
- Trends over snapshots — show the trajectory
- Flag risks early and quantify the gap
- Include both leading indicators (pipeline) and lagging indicators (bookings)
- Charts for trends, tables for financial summaries
