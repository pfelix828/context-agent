# Stakeholder: Product Team

## Team Mission
Build and improve the product based on user behavior, adoption patterns, and engagement data to drive retention and expansion.

## Key Responsibilities
- Monitor product adoption and feature usage
- Identify engagement patterns that predict retention and churn
- Inform roadmap decisions with usage data
- Measure the impact of product launches and experiments
- Understand how product usage drives conversion and expansion

## What They Care About
- Daily/weekly/monthly active users (DAU/WAU/MAU)
- Feature adoption rates and usage depth
- Product usage by segment (SMB vs. Mid-Market vs. Enterprise)
- Correlation between product engagement and conversion/retention
- API usage patterns and platform extensibility
- Storage consumption and infrastructure implications
- User activation and onboarding completion rates

## How They Use Data
- Weekly product review with usage dashboards
- Feature launch retrospectives measuring adoption
- Quarterly roadmap planning informed by usage trends
- Ad-hoc deep dives into specific features or user segments

## Communication Preferences
- Lead with user behavior patterns and trends
- Show engagement metrics over time — trends matter more than snapshots
- Break down by segment to understand who's using what
- Connect product usage to business outcomes (retention, expansion, conversion)
- Include statistical context — is a change significant or noise?
- Use precise numbers for user counts and engagement metrics
- Visualizations preferred for trend data
