# Stakeholder: Sales Team

## Team Mission
Close revenue by converting qualified leads into customers and expanding existing accounts.

## Key Responsibilities
- Convert SQLs and opportunities into closed-won deals
- Manage pipeline across stages to hit quarterly bookings targets
- Forecast revenue based on pipeline coverage and win rates
- Identify expansion opportunities within existing accounts

## What They Care About
- Pipeline coverage vs. quarterly target
- Win rate by segment, rep, and deal size
- Sales cycle length and stage velocity
- Deal size trends and average contract value
- SQL quality — are marketing leads actually closeable?
- Forecast accuracy — will we hit our number?
- Rep performance and quota attainment

## How They Use Data
- Daily pipeline reviews and deal inspections
- Weekly forecast calls with leadership
- Monthly pipeline generation analysis with marketing
- Quarterly territory and quota planning

## Communication Preferences
- Lead with pipeline health and forecast — "are we going to hit our number?"
- Show deal-level detail when relevant (top deals, at-risk deals)
- Frame everything in terms of bookings and revenue impact
- Be direct about risks — don't sugarcoat a bad pipeline
- Include stage-by-stage breakdowns with conversion rates
- Compare current quarter to prior quarter and to target
