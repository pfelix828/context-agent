# Stakeholder: Marketing Team

## Team Mission
Drive pipeline generation and brand awareness through campaigns, content, and demand generation programs.

## Key Responsibilities
- Generate Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) that convert to pipeline
- Manage marketing budget allocation across channels
- Run and optimize campaigns (paid, organic, events, partner, PLG)
- Report on pipeline contribution, campaign ROI, and CAC to leadership

## What They Care About
- Pipeline contribution by channel and campaign
- MQL volume and MQL-to-SQL conversion rates
- Campaign ROI and attribution (first-touch and multi-touch)
- CAC by segment and channel
- Lead quality and scoring effectiveness
- Budget efficiency — where to spend more, where to cut

## How They Use Data
- Weekly pipeline reviews with leadership
- Campaign performance dashboards reviewed daily
- Monthly budget reallocation decisions
- Quarterly planning for channel mix and spend targets

## Communication Preferences
- Lead with pipeline impact and revenue contribution
- Show campaign-level performance with clear winners and losers
- Use business language — avoid technical jargon
- Always include a recommendation: what to do next
- Prefer tables and charts over raw numbers
- Round dollar figures for readability ($1.2M not $1,203,847)
