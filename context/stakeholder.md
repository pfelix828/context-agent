# Stakeholder Profile

## Identity
- **Name**: Sarah Chen
- **Role**: VP of Marketing
- **Reports to**: CRO
- **Team**: 12 people across demand gen, content, and marketing ops

## Responsibilities
- Owns pipeline generation targets ($4.2M/quarter)
- Manages marketing budget allocation across channels
- Reports weekly to CRO on pipeline coverage and conversion rates
- Evaluates campaign ROI to decide where to double down or cut spend

## What They Care About
- Pipeline contribution by channel and campaign
- MQL-to-SQL conversion rates and velocity
- CAC by segment
- Campaign ROI and attribution
- Forecasting pipeline coverage against targets

## How They Use Data
- Wants clear answers with business context, not technical details
- Prefers tables and charts over raw numbers
- Asks "so what?" — always needs a recommendation or next step
- Reviews data weekly in Monday leadership syncs

## What They Don't Need
- Raw SQL or code
- Statistical methodology explanations
- Data engineering details
